# Semantic Entity Map — Government Jobs India

This file defines entity relationships for major government exam topics. Use this to guide semantic content creation, internal linking, and topical authority building.

---

## 1. SSC (Staff Selection Commission)

### Entity: SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level)
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** Tier 1 Exam, Tier 2 Exam, Graduate Level Exam, SSC CHSL, SSC GD
- **Posts:** Tax Assistant, Auditor, Inspector (Income Tax), Sub Inspector (CBI), Assistant Section Officer (ASO), Statistical Investigator
- **Selection Stages:** Tier 1 (CBT), Tier 2 (CBT), Document Verification
- **Qualification:** Graduation from recognized university

### Entity: SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level)
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CGL, SSC MTS, 12th Pass Jobs
- **Posts:** Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Postal Assistant, Sorting Assistant, Data Entry Operator (DEO), Court Clerk
- **Selection Stages:** Tier 1 (CBT), Tier 2 (Descriptive), Typing Test/Skill Test
- **Qualification:** 12th Pass (Higher Secondary)

### Entity: SSC MTS (Multi Tasking Staff)
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CHSL, SSC GD, 10th Pass Jobs
- **Posts:** Peon, Watchman, Gardener, Safaiwala, Multi Tasking Staff
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
- **Qualification:** 10th Pass (Matriculation)

### Entity: SSC GD Constable
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC MTS, CRPF, BSF, CISF, ITBP, SSB, NIA
- **Posts:** Constable (GD) in CAPFs, NIA, SSF, Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), Medical Examination
- **Qualification:** 10th Pass

### Entity: SSC CPO (Central Police Organisation)
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CGL, Delhi Police, CAPF
- **Posts:** Sub Inspector in Delhi Police, Sub Inspector in CAPF
- **Selection Stages:** Paper I, Paper II, Physical Standard Test, Physical Endurance Test, Medical Examination
- **Qualification:** Graduation

### Entity: SSC JE (Junior Engineer)
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CGL, Engineering Jobs, CPWD, CWC, MES
- **Posts:** Junior Engineer (Civil), Junior Engineer (Electrical), Junior Engineer (Mechanical), Junior Engineer (Quantity Surveying & Contracts)
- **Selection Stages:** Paper I (CBT), Paper II (CBT)
- **Qualification:** Diploma/Degree in Engineering

### Entity: SSC Stenographer
- **Parent Entity:** Staff Selection Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CHSL, Typing Jobs
- **Posts:** Stenographer Grade C, Stenographer Grade D
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Skill Test (Stenography)
- **Qualification:** 12th Pass

## 2. Railway (Indian Railways)

### Entity: RRB NTPC (Non-Technical Popular Categories)
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Recruitment Board
- **Related Entities:** RRB Group D, RRB ALP, Indian Railways
- **Posts:** Station Master, Goods Guard, Traffic Assistant, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Account Assistant cum Typist, Junior Clerk cum Typist
- **Selection Stages:** CBT 1, CBT 2, Typing Skill Test/CBAT, Document Verification
- **Qualification:** Graduation (for higher posts), 12th Pass (for lower posts)

### Entity: RRB Group D
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Recruitment Board
- **Related Entities:** RRB NTPC, RRB ALP, 10th Pass Jobs
- **Posts:** Track Maintainer Grade IV, Helper/Assistant in various departments, Pointsman, Gateman
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Document Verification
- **Qualification:** 10th Pass + ITI (in some cases)

### Entity: RRB ALP (Assistant Loco Pilot)
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Recruitment Board
- **Related Entities:** RRB NTPC, RRB Group D, RRB Technician
- **Posts:** Assistant Loco Pilot, Technician Grade III
- **Selection Stages:** CBT 1, CBT 2, CBAT (Computer Based Aptitude Test), Document Verification
- **Qualification:** 10th Pass + ITI/Diploma in Engineering

### Entity: RRB JE (Junior Engineer)
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Recruitment Board
- **Related Entities:** SSC JE, Engineering Jobs
- **Posts:** Junior Engineer (Civil), Junior Engineer (Electrical), Junior Engineer (Mechanical), Depot Material Superintendent, Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant
- **Selection Stages:** CBT 1, CBT 2, Document Verification
- **Qualification:** Diploma/Degree in Engineering

### Entity: RPF Constable
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Protection Force
- **Related Entities:** RPF SI, SSC GD, CRPF
- **Posts:** Constable (Male), Constable (Female)
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Physical Efficiency Test, Physical Measurement Test, Document Verification
- **Qualification:** 10th Pass

### Entity: RPF SI (Sub Inspector)
- **Parent Entity:** Railway Protection Force
- **Related Entities:** RPF Constable, SSC CPO
- **Posts:** Sub Inspector (Male), Sub Inspector (Female)
- **Selection Stages:** CBT, Physical Efficiency Test, Physical Measurement Test, Document Verification
- **Qualification:** Graduation

## 4. UPSC (Union Public Service Commission)

### Entity: UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS)
- **Parent Entity:** Union Public Service Commission
- **Related Entities:** UPSC CDS, UPSC NDA, State PSC
- **Posts:** IAS (Indian Administrative Service), IPS (Indian Police Service), IFS (Indian Foreign Service), IRS (Indian Revenue Service), IRTS, IDAS, IDES, and 20+ other services
- **Selection Stages:** Preliminary Exam (CSAT), Main Exam (9 papers), Personality Test (Interview)
- **Qualification:** Graduation from recognized university

### Entity: UPSC CDS (Combined Defence Services)
- **Parent Entity:** Union Public Service Commission
- **Related Entities:** UPSC NDA, Indian Army, Indian Navy, Indian Air Force, OTA
- **Posts:** IMA (Indian Military Academy), INA (Indian Naval Academy), AFA (Air Force Academy), OTA (Officers Training Academy)
- **Selection Stages:** Written Exam, SSB Interview (5 days)
- **Qualification:** Graduation (specific requirements per academy)

### Entity: UPSC NDA (National Defence Academy)
- **Parent Entity:** Union Public Service Commission
- **Related Entities:** UPSC CDS, Indian Army, Indian Navy, Indian Air Force
- **Posts:** Entry to National Defence Academy for Army/Navy/Air Force wings
- **Selection Stages:** Written Exam, SSB Interview (5 days)
- **Qualification:** 12th Pass (10+2)

### Entity: UPSC CAPF (Central Armed Police Forces)
- **Parent Entity:** Union Public Service Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC CPO, CRPF, BSF, CISF, ITBP, SSB
- **Posts:** Assistant Commandant in CRPF, BSF, CISF, ITBP, SSB
- **Selection Stages:** Written Exam, Physical Standards Test, Physical Efficiency Test, Medical Examination, Interview
- **Qualification:** Graduation

### Entity: UPSC ESE (Engineering Services Exam)
- **Parent Entity:** Union Public Service Commission
- **Related Entities:** SSC JE, RRB JE, GATE
- **Posts:** Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
- **Selection Stages:** Preliminary Exam, Main Exam, Personality Test
- **Qualification:** Engineering Degree (B.E./B.Tech)

### Entity: DRDO (Defence Research & Development Organisation)

- **Posts:** Scientist B, Scientist C, CEPTAM (Technician A, Senior Technical Assistant), Apprentice

## 6. Teaching

### Entity: CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test)
- **Parent Entity:** CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education)
- **Related Entities:** State TET, KVS, NVS, DSSSB Teacher
- **Posts:** Eligibility certification for Classes I-V (Paper I) and Classes VI-VIII (Paper II)
- **Qualification:** D.El.Ed/B.El.Ed/B.Ed

### Entity: KVS (Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan)
- **Parent Entity:** Ministry of Education
- **Related Entities:** NVS, CTET, DSSSB Teacher
- **Posts:** PRT (Primary Teacher), TGT (Trained Graduate Teacher), PGT (Post Graduate Teacher), Librarian, Principal, Vice Principal
- **Selection:** Written Exam, Interview (for teaching posts)
- **Qualification:** D.El.Ed + CTET (PRT), B.Ed + CTET (TGT), M.A./M.Sc + B.Ed (PGT)

### Entity: NVS (Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti)
- **Parent Entity:** Ministry of Education
- **Related Entities:** KVS, CTET, JNV
- **Posts:** PGT, TGT, Miscellaneous Category (Staff Nurse, Catering Assistant, etc.), LDC, Non-Teaching Staff
- **Selection:** Written Exam, Interview
- **Qualification:** Same as KVS for teaching posts

### Entity: UGC NET (National Eligibility Test)
- **Parent Entity:** University Grants Commission / NTA
- **Related Entities:** CSIR NET, SET, Assistant Professor Jobs
- **Posts:** Eligibility for Assistant Professor, JRF (Junior Research Fellowship)
- **Selection:** Paper I (General), Paper II (Subject-specific)
- **Qualification:** Post-graduation with 55% marks

### Entity: State TET
- **Parent Entity:** Respective State Education Departments
- **Related Entities:** CTET, UPTET, MPTET, REET, HTET, BTET, OTET
- **Posts:** Primary Teacher, Upper Primary Teacher in state government schools
- **Qualification:** D.El.Ed/B.El.Ed/B.Ed
- **Key State TETs:** UPTET, MPTET, REET (Rajasthan), HTET (Haryana), BTET (Bihar), OTET (Odisha), TNTET (Tamil Nadu), APTET (Andhra Pradesh), WBTET (West Bengal)

### Entity: DSSSB Teacher
- **Parent Entity:** Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board
- **Related Entities:** CTET, KVS, Delhi Govt Schools
- **Posts:** PRT, TGT, PGT, Nursery Teacher, Physical Education Teacher, Special Education Teacher
- **Selection:** One-Tier/Two-Tier CBT
- **Qualification:** Varies by post + CTET/DSSSB qualifying

## 8. Without Exam (Direct Recruitment / Merit-Based)

### Entity: Govt Jobs Without Exam
- **Parent Entity:** Various Government Departments
- **Related Entities:** Direct Recruitment, Merit-Based Selection, Interview-Only Jobs, Walk-In Jobs
- **Selection Methods:** Merit list (academic percentage), Interview only, Document verification, Skill test only
- **Common Departments:** District Courts, Municipal Corporations, State Health Departments, Gram Panchayats, PSUs

### Entity: Anganwadi Recruitment
- **Parent Entity:** Ministry of Women & Child Development
- **Related Entities:** Govt Jobs Without Exam, 8th Pass Jobs, 10th Pass Jobs
- **Posts:** Anganwadi Worker, Anganwadi Helper, Mini Anganwadi Worker, Anganwadi Supervisor
- **Selection:** Merit-based (academic marks) + Interview
- **Qualification:** 8th Pass (Helper), 10th/12th Pass (Worker)

### Entity: Court Peon / Process Server
- **Parent Entity:** District & Sessions Courts
- **Related Entities:** Govt Jobs Without Exam, 8th Pass Jobs
- **Posts:** Peon, Process Server, Chowkidar, Sweeper, Liftman
- **Selection:** Merit-based / Walk-in Interview
- **Qualification:** 8th Pass / 10th Pass

### Entity: Gram Panchayat / Block Level Jobs
- **Parent Entity:** State Rural Development Departments
- **Related Entities:** Govt Jobs Without Exam, State Government Jobs
- **Posts:** Gram Rozgar Sahayak, Panchayat Secretary, Data Entry Operator, BDO Office Staff, MGNREGA Worker
- **Selection:** Merit + Interview / Walk-in
- **Qualification:** 10th/12th Pass / Graduation
