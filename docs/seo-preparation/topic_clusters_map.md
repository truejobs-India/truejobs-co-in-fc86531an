# Topic Clusters Map — Government Jobs India

## Cluster Priority Ranking

| Tier | Clusters | Monthly Search Volume (Est.) | Rationale |
|------|----------|------------------------------|-----------|
| **Priority 1** | SSC, Railway | 5M+ combined | Highest search volume, broadest audience (10th/12th/Graduate), multiple exams per year, massive vacancy counts |
| **Priority 2** | Banking, UPSC | 3M+ combined | Strong volume, aspirational audience with high engagement, competitive but high-value content |
| **Priority 3** | Defence, Teaching, State PSC, Without Exam | 2M+ combined | Niche but valuable long-tail, less competitive, loyal audience segments |

**Publishing order:** Priority 1 clusters first → Priority 2 → Priority 3

## Cross-Cluster Linking Rules

1. **Every supporting page** links to its authority page AND the cluster hub
2. **Every authority page** links to the cluster hub AND at least 2 other authority pages in the same cluster
3. **Every cluster hub** links to all other cluster hubs AND `/sarkari-jobs` (site-wide hub)
4. **Qualification-based cross-links:** Pages targeting "10th pass" link across clusters (SSC MTS ↔ RRB Group D ↔ Agniveer ↔ Without Exam)
5. **Exam-type cross-links:** Engineering exams link across clusters (SSC JE ↔ RRB JE ↔ UPSC ESE)
